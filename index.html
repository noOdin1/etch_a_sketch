Etch-a-Sketch

 

 

Instruction:

Drawing area is the area surrounded by the light red border. Move the mouse over the drawing area to color it. Normal mode is the default mode. Default mode will make the cells black, while random mode mode will change the cells to a random color. Change modes by clicking outside the drawing area.

Click on "Resize Area" to change the number of cells in the drawing area.

Click on the colored cells to darken it.

Press Shift key while moving over the cells will reset the cells colors.
